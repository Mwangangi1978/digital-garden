## Overview

A game built around the idea that the world is held together by systems more fragile than they look—and that small choices compound. This is the story of designing and building it.

## The idea

I wanted the core mechanic itself to carry the theme: players make decisions that feel local and small, then watch them ripple outward in ways that aren't easy to take back. Fragility as a feeling, not just a label.

## What I built

- The core loop and the rules that make decisions feel consequential.
- The systems underneath—state, progression, and the feedback that tells players what their choices did.
- The interface and feel: the moment-to-moment interactions that make the world feel alive and breakable.
