Hi!

This is my Substack. I just started it - but in truth, these thoughts have been flying around in my head for a while now. They finally have somewhere to land.

Here, you'll find stories about small moments that become massive in your head. About flights I never took, conversations I never finished and questions I still ask myself in the tub with the water running.

Some pieces will be abstract. Some are painfully clear. Some written at 1 AM when you shouldn't be writing anything at all. But all of it is me, and maybe - you'll find pieces of yourself in them too.

I hope this space becomes a soft window seat. Or a place where the seatbelt sign is always off. Or just somewhere to land.

Thanks for being here.
